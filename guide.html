Leaf Essentials Guidelines

Server Guidelines

  • Be respectful to all players and staff.
  • No cheating, exploiting, or hacking.
  • Keep chat appropriate and avoid spam.
  • Follow instructions from admins and moderators.
  • Report any rule-breaking through the report system.

Suggestions

Submit your ideas to improve Leaf Essentials! Your suggestions will be sent directly to our Discord server.

Appeals

Submit an appeal for any bans or issues you’ve encountered. Appeals are sent directly to our staff Discord.
